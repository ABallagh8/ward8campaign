My Vision for Ward 8

As Ward 8 continues to grow, we must ensure that progress benefits all of us — not just developers or political insiders. I’m committed to delivering bold, inclusive, and transparent leadership that puts people first.

  • 🏘️ Housing for Real People: Expand non-market and affordable rental options. Push for gentle density (like duplexes and townhomes) to make housing attainable for families, students, and newcomers alike.
  • 🚍 Transit That Works: Improve east-west bus routes, increase weekend service, and invest in safer pedestrian infrastructure. We need streets designed for everyone — not just cars.
  • 🌿 A Greener Ward 8: Protect our tree canopy, fight for energy-efficient retrofits, and make our neighbourhoods more climate-resilient — starting right here, not “someday.”
  • 📣 A Councillor Who Listens: I’ll hold regular town halls, post all my council votes, and empower residents to help shape our ward’s budget priorities through participatory tools.
  • 💡 Equity at the Centre: One in four residents here identifies as a visible minority. That diversity should be represented, celebrated, and protected in policy decisions — not sidelined.

This campaign is about us — the people who live here, work here, raise families here. Let’s build the Hamilton we deserve, together.
