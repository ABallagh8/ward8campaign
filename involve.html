Get Involved

This campaign isn’t just about one candidate — it’s about building a movement for a better Ward 8. Your voice, your time, and your passion can make the difference between business as usual and real, people-powered change. Here’s how you can be part of it:

  • Volunteer: Whether you’re a seasoned campaigner or brand new to politics, we’ve got a role for you! Knock doors and connect with neighbours Make phone calls to mobilize voters Help out at events and community gatherings Contribute your skills behind the scenes (design, data, logistics, you name it!)
  • Donate: Every dollar supports outreach, materials, and mobilization efforts.
  • Endorse Alex: Lend your voice to the cause! Share why you’re backing Alex and help us reach more people. Personal endorsements — whether on social media, at community events, or among friends — are one of the most powerful tools we have.

Want to know more? Have an idea or a skill to offer? Ready to dive in? Head to the Contact page and fill out the form — we’ll be in touch ASAP to get you plugged in. Together, we can shake up the status quo and build a Ward 8 that works for everyone. Let’s make it happen — join us today!
