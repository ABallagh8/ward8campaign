Meet Alex

I’m Alex Ballagh — a project manager, community organizer, and lifelong Hamiltonian. I’m running for City Councillor in Ward 8 because I believe our city deserves more transparent leadership, bolder policy, and a council that listens. With a background in research, public service, and grassroots advocacy, I’ve spent years solving problems, building partnerships, and fighting for the change our communities need.

  • Master of Medical Science & PMP Certified
  • Community volunteer and social services advocate
  • Lead organizer for city-wide engagement and fundraising campaigns
  • Focused on affordability, transit, housing, and climate justice

Community Work

My campaign is grounded in the lived experience of working with and for the community. Here are a few of the initiatives I've led or been deeply involved in:

  • Fundraising for the NDP National Convention: Organized a successful concert fundraiser to help bring diverse voices to the national stage.
  • Tenant-Led Response Initiatives: Coordinated community-led strategies for overdose response and housing advocacy in nonprofit and student housing sectors.
  • Community Engagement Research: Developed toolkits to support NDP riding associations in recruiting and retaining volunteers, improving local participation in democratic processes.
  • Graduate Student Leadership: Served as VP of the McMaster Graduate Student Association, advocating for better services, mental health supports, and fair funding for students.
  • Grassroots Campaigning: Worked on civic engagement projects across Hamilton focused on climate, justice, and equitable development.

ACORN Advocacy

  • Elected Co-Chair of the Hamilton ACORN Downtown Chapter
  • Passed anti-renoviction and anti-demoviction bylaws — the first in Ontario
  • Organized and spoke at national and local protests, conventions, and community events
  • Unionized tenants and fought for improved housing conditions across Hamilton
  • Led public delegations and advocacy campaigns to shape policy
