Community Work

Alex Ballagh has a long-standing commitment to grassroots advocacy and community leadership. From organizing national fundraisers to securing tenant rights and mental health coverage, Alex's work has touched lives across Hamilton and beyond.

Graduate Student Leadership

  • Vice President of the Graduate Student Association (2017-2018): Reinstated and expanded mental health coverage via Empower Me program; protected critical services from future cuts.
  • Founding Chair of the Graduate Clubs Committee: Launched inclusive student clubs and built governing framework for club activities.
  • Chair of the Sports Leagues Committee: Oversaw budgets, scheduling, and recreation for student-led sports leagues.

Housing Justice & ACORN Organizing

  • Co-Chair of Hamilton ACORN Downtown Chapter and lead organizer of city-wide protests and actions.
  • Instrumental in passing Ontario’s first anti-renoviction and anti-demoviction bylaws (2024).
  • Led tenant unionization efforts at 27 Bold Street and 103 Emerald Street.
  • Speaker at national housing events and rallies, including hosting MPs and MPPs at ACORN gatherings.

Community Campaigns & Organizing

  • Hamilton Alliance for Tiny Shelters (HATS): Organized high-impact fundraisers like the Open House and documentary screening with Khaleel Seivwright.
  • YWCA: Coordinator and photographer for major events like Walk a Mile in Her Shoes and Women of Distinction Gala.
  • Ontario Health Coalition: March organizer, canvasser, and healthcare advocate against privatization.
  • Unifor 5555: Member of the Political Action and Bylaw Committees; led women’s hygiene product drives and safety initiatives.

Political Leadership & Democratic Renewal

  • Chair of Federal Election Planning Committee (Hamilton West-Ancaster-Dundas): Managed campaign operations, volunteers, and budget coordination.
  • National Organizing: Created and distributed election toolkits adopted federally by the NDP for volunteer management, union engagement, and nomination prep.

National Convention Organizer

  • Organized "Here’s to Hamilton" concert fundraiser raising $25,000+ for the NDP National Convention with LT the Monk and Harrison Kennedy.
  • Coordinated the “Cocktail Hour with Singh and Stiles” VIP reception.
  • Served as delegate and merchandising lead at the convention.

Additional Community Engagement

  • Pride Hamilton: Setup/takedown lead and vendor coordinator for the annual festival.
  • St. Joseph’s Hospital Volunteer (2016–2019): Assisted with senior care, dialysis, endoscopy, and music-based wellness programs.
  • Black Throne Productions (2024): Helped organize the La Rhune Mountain Doomfest to support the local music scene.
