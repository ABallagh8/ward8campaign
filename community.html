Community Work

Grassroots Campaigning

I’ve organized concerts, canvasses, and policy roundtables that bring people together to solve problems and build power — from student housing to mental health and climate justice.

Student Advocacy

As Vice President of the McMaster Graduate Student Association, I fought for fair funding, better services, and meaningful mental health supports for our academic community.

Democratic Renewal

I authored and distributed toolkits to help local NDP riding associations recruit, engage, and retain grassroots volunteers — work that’s since empowered dozens of new local leaders.

Tenant Organizing & Housing Justice with ACORN

Co-Chairing Hamilton ACORN

As Co-Chair of the Downtown Chapter, I’ve helped tenants organize, fight back, and win against some of the worst slumlords in the country — including at 27 Bold and 103 Emerald.

Winning Anti-Renoviction Protections

I helped ACORN pass Ontario’s first-ever anti-renoviction and anti-demoviction bylaw, protecting vulnerable renters from displacement across the city.

Direct Action & Solidarity

From maximum heat protests to LCBO solidarity marches and hosting elected officials face-to-face with tenants — I’ve always believed that showing up together is the first step toward change.

Outreach & Organizing

I’ve led ACORN outreach at libraries, Art Crawl, May Day, and Labour Day. I also helped rebuild the Hamilton Mountain chapter and organized annual picnics to strengthen our movement.
