A Vision for Ward 8

  • Affordable Housing for All: Increase the supply of non-market and purpose-built rentals, and push for stronger tenant protections.
  • Safer, Connected Streets: Invest in safer intersections, complete sidewalks, and improved transit access for all residents.
  • Transparent, Accountable Governance: End backroom politics and bring more decisions into the public eye with participatory budgeting and ward-wide consultations.
  • Climate Action Starts Here: Champion green infrastructure, expand the tree canopy, and prioritize clean, efficient transit solutions.
  • Support for Seniors & Youth: Expand community services, youth programming, and support aging-in-place strategies.
